About Boomtown Land-works

Boomtown Land-works is a veteran-owned, family-run land services company serving Snohomish County, WA. Specializing in land clearing, property preparation, grading, and brush removal, we bring military precision and family values to every job. From overgrown lots to new construction sites, we get your land ready — on time and on budget.

Our Work

Land Clearing

Complete clearing of overgrown lots, trees, and vegetation to prepare your property for its next chapter.

Grading & Leveling

Precision grading and earth-moving to create a level, stable foundation for construction or landscaping.

Brush Removal

Fast, thorough removal of brush, blackberries, and invasive growth from any size property.

Property Preparation

Full-service site prep for new builds, driveways, septic systems, and more.

Stump & Root Removal

Complete stump grinding and root extraction so your land is truly clear and ready.

Erosion Control & Drainage

Proper grading and drainage solutions to protect your property from water damage.
